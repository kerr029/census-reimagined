Workspace Furniture Feedback

CENSUS REIMAGINED

The purpose for asking your feedback is to ensure we hear you and provide this opportunity to weigh in on possible workspace and furniture options for our future work environment. We'll explain the space and furniture, including the function, and ask direct questions for your feedback. Together, we'll create a better work environment for everyone.

Our space to...

CREATE, CONTRIBUTE, & CONNECT

Designing our next-generation work environment requires a team effort. Your opinion counts. By offering feedback you help us determine our future workspace requirements to ensure we effectively execute our core mission.

Census Reimagined has 3 core tenants: Purpose, People, Planet. Help us keep our tenants front and center while we design workspaces that you want and which support the work you do now and in the future.

Our goal is to design a flexible work environment which offers both collaborative space for dynamic group work as well as quiet space for focused deep work. Thank you for your time and support!

Not a ‘one size fits all’ approach

WORKSPACES

Census Reimagined is based on a holistic approach to designing the next-generation work environment focused on leveraging activity-based design to meet staff needs both cognitively/mentally and physically.

Flexibility and having choices, such as where to work, how to work, and when to work, is key. We want to promote both effectiveness and wellness.

We look forward to receiving your feedback.

Did you know?

A recent report found when offering a flexible, hybrid work environment comprised of both collaborative and quiet/head-down space, 41% report better problem solving and 39% report increased team productivity. (US Workplace Survey 2020 Summer/Fall, Gensler Research Institute)

PRIVATE SPACES

Census Reimagined is dedicated to providing ample private space.

The Phone Booth/Rooms serve as an enclosed space for periods of short term work or brief, private conversations. The spaces are intended to be quiet, private, and free from disturbance.

Did you know?

Organizations offering a broader range of spaces—for collaboration, socializing, focus, rejuvenation and privacy—tend to have more engaged employees. (Steelcase 360 Report Engagement and the Global Workplace)
